About the BRAINS AI Lab

The BRAINS Lab (Broad Research in AI and Next-gen Systems) at IEDUP fosters innovation across Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and Smart Industry 4.0. In collaboration with SIDBI, it supports MSMEs and startups with digital transformation, AI-based diagnostics, and automation solutions.
