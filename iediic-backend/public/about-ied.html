About IEDUP

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Building Future-Ready Startups • Driving Innovation in Uttar Pradesh

Institute of Entrepreneurship Development (IED UP) , Lucknow

Institute for Entrepreneurship Development, Uttar Pradesh (IEDUP) Premier Institute and Centre of Excellence in the field of entrepreneurship promotion and Human Resource Development, recognized by State Government. Institute, as an autonomous organization under Department of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises & Export Promotion, Govt. of U.P., was set up in 1986. IEDUP's Governing Board, headed by Principal Secretary, MSME & Export Promotion, Govt. of U.P., comprises of senior bureaucrats, bankers, academicians and eminent professionals.

Institute of Entrepreneurship Development, U.P. (IEDUP), Lucknow, is a State Level Institute has State of the Art infrastructure, best among other State level ED Institutions, sprawled over 5 acres of land having well-equipped training and hostel facilities. IEDUP is located at Sarojini Nagar industrial area, Lucknow which has about 70-80 industries in various sectors.
