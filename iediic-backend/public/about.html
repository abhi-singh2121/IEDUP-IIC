About IIC

At IED UP’s Incubation Innovation Centre (IIC), we are proud to host India’s first Export-Specific Incubation Centre, transforming Uttar Pradesh into a global hub for export-oriented growth. In collaboration with Itech Innovation Foundation, we empower MSMEs, startups, and entrepreneurs with advanced management practices, technology integration, and global market intelligence.

Our Vision

To position Uttar Pradesh as a leading export-driven economy by nurturing globally competitive enterprises and strengthening India’s presence in international markets.

Our Mission

We aim to create a complete ecosystem supporting export-led entrepreneurship – from idea validation and product development to international market access and trade facilitation.

What We Do

Incubation & Mentorship

Expert mentorship, business modelling, and capacity-building for export-focused startups.

Technology Integration

Access to Industry 4.0 tools, digital solutions, and advanced productivity tech.

Market Access & Networking

Connections with export councils, EXIM Bank, industry partners, and global buyers.

Policy & Compliance Guidance

Support for export documentation, regulations, and government incentives.

Skill Development & Training

Workshops on export management, quality standards, and international marketing.
