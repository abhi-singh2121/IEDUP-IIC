Training & Entrepreneurship Development Programs

ITech delivers impactful entrepreneurship programs through hands-on workshops, sustainability-driven projects, and startup ecosystem initiatives that inspire innovation and real-world business creation.

Marathon Entrepreneurship Workshop

The Marathon Entrepreneurship Workshop is a record-setting initiative that combines intensive business ideation, strategy design, and startup readiness under one continuous learning format. Participants go through structured mentorship, pitching sessions, and real-time business simulations designed to spark entrepreneurial thinking.

It has engaged thousands of students and aspiring entrepreneurs, creating an immersive platform that connects academic learning to practical startup execution.

SDG Entrepreneurship Workshop

In partnership with WWF and aligned with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals, this workshop promotes sustainability-focused entrepreneurship. Participants learn to build business models that address water conservation, renewable resources, and climate-smart innovation.

The workshop inspires young innovators to transform environmental responsibility into viable business opportunities.

Entrepreneurs Café

Entrepreneurs Café is a collaborative networking platform connecting founders, mentors, investors, and industry professionals. Through open discussions and interactive sessions, it fosters idea exchange, startup mentoring, and growth opportunities for early-stage entrepreneurs.

Startup Safar

Startup Safar celebrates entrepreneurial journeys through real-life stories of founders. These sessions bring authentic experiences to students and startup enthusiasts, providing insights into problem-solving, innovation, and resilience.

Startup Nexus

Startup Nexus is ITech’s annual startup conclave that brings together investors, startups, academia, and government. The event features startup pitching, panel discussions, ideathons, and showcases that foster meaningful collaboration within the innovation ecosystem.

SIMP — Smart Industry Managers Program

The SIMP program bridges the gap between academia and industry through practical management exposure, industrial visits, and hands-on project learning. It equips participants with essential skills to manage startups, MSMEs, and corporate initiatives effectively.
